About IDR

Where Industry Expertise
Meets Academic Rigour

The Institute of Digital Risk is an industry-led training and deployment institute built for digital, cyber and technology risk roles in regulated, high-consequence environments. We bridge the critical gap between theoretical knowledge and operational readiness — producing practitioners who are deployable from day one.

Anchored by a formal UK university partnership, IDR blends academic insight with live industry projects, sector-specific frameworks and real-world risk scenarios. Our programmes prepare students and professionals alike for the most demanding roles in financial services, critical infrastructure and beyond.
